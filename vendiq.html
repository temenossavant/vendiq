Common questions
Do I need to pay for the API key?
Anthropic offers a free tier. For light daily use, the free credits are typically enough to get started. Visit console.anthropic.com to sign up.
Are the prices 100% accurate?
AI search gives strong estimates, but always verify before purchasing — especially bulk prices, which vary by supplier and quantity. Use the scan feature for more precise numbers from actual listings.
What is Amazon FBA?
Fulfilled by Amazon (FBA) means you ship your products to Amazon's warehouse, and they handle storage, packing, shipping, and customer service. You pay fees, but it's largely hands-off.
Where do I actually buy in bulk?
Good starting places: Alibaba.com, Sam's Club, Costco Business Delivery, Faire, and wholesale directories like WorldwideBrands. Always order a small test batch first.
How do I install Vendiq as an app?
On iPhone: open in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android: open in Chrome → menu → Add to Home Screen. It works like a native app with no App Store needed.
