Common questions
What is Amazon FBA?
Fulfilled by Amazon — you ship products to their warehouse, they handle storage, packing, and shipping. You pay fees but it's hands-off.
Where do I buy in bulk?
Great starting points: Alibaba.com, Sam's Club, Costco Business Delivery, and Faire. Always order a small test batch first.
Are the AI prices accurate?
They're strong estimates — always verify before purchasing, especially bulk prices which vary by supplier and quantity.
How do I install Vendiq as an app?
iPhone: open in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen. Android: Chrome menu → Add to Home Screen.
