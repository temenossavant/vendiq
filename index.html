The workflow

From search to
shipped inventory

1

Search any product

Type a product name into the Scout tab. Vendiq's AI searches Amazon and wholesale suppliers simultaneously and autofills both prices in seconds.

2

Scan a bulk deal

Browsing Alibaba or Sam's Club? Screenshot the listing and tap "Scan." Vendiq reads the bulk price directly from the image — no typing needed.

3

Review your profit breakdown

See exactly what you'll earn per unit and in total, after every Amazon fee. Get a clear Buy / Skip / Borderline verdict against your margin target.

4

Add to tracker with notes

Log the deal with a category, supplier notes, and attached screenshots. Tap any product in the Tracker to expand its full deal history.

5

Monitor & export

Track total profit, invested, and avg margin across all your deals. Filter by category. Export to CSV anytime for use in a spreadsheet.
